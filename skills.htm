Online Business Skills

Online business is done through Amazon, Shopify or Udemy. These websites allow you to set up your own online stores, upload your products and start your online business from the comfort of your home. However, keep in mind that once you have created a store, making it successful is entirely up to your efforts + your naseeb. From marketing to advertising, from taking customer orders to delivering the products to their homes, you are responsible for everything. Whether you handle all these tasks yourself or build your own team and have them do the work, or hire a company, your customer must be 100% satisfied with both your service and products after the purchase, otherwise the business is doomed to failure.

Freelancing Skills

Freelancing is the art of working independently as a self-employed individual, offering your skills and expertise directly to clients without relying solely on platforms like Fiverr or Upwork. True freelancing is about building your personal brand, networking, and cultivating relationships to secure clients on your own terms. It’s a business where you operate as a service provider, managing everything from client acquisition to project delivery, pricing, and invoicing. This approach provides full control over your work, allowing you to create a sustainable and flexible career while establishing yourself as a trusted professional in your field.
