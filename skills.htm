Learn Business Skills

You can start an online business through platforms like Amazon, Shopify, or Udemy. These websites let you set up your online stores, upload your products, and launch your business from the comfort of your home. However, keep in mind that after setting up your store, making it successful depends entirely on your own hard work and luck. From marketing and advertising to taking customer orders and delivering products to their homes, all these responsibilities fall on you. Whether you handle everything yourself, build a team to help, or hire a company, your customers must be 100% satisfied with both your service and products after purchase—otherwise, your business is bound to fail.

Learn Feelancing Skills

This is a bit different from running your own individual business because on freelancing platforms, you don’t get to talk directly to your clients unless they respond to you first. Until then, you have to wait. But if you run your own business using social media as your medium, you can easily reach your target customers directly, talk to them, and offer your services.

Here, you don’t have to compete with others like on freelancing platforms where everyone is lined up writing proposals. You can connect with people on Facebook, Google, YouTube, LinkedIn, Quora, and offer your services to them. Don’t get discouraged if you get rejected. Keep moving forward, and inshaAllah, you will succeed.

Once you find the formula to get direct clients that works for you and suits your style, stick to it, improve it, and start getting one or two more clients. If you also enjoy teaching alongside freelancing, find your own students and teach them freelancing. This way, you hit two targets with one arrow and increase your income.

This method gives you full control over your business and helps you become recognized as a reliable professional.

Learn Platforms Skills

On platforms like Fiverr, Upwork, Guru, Freelancer.com, freelancers create services or apply to different projects. You wait for a response, then accept the project. After completing the work, you deliver it and get paid.

Here, you’re competing with other freelancers. You need excellent communication skills. Speak confidently and with courage. Apply only for projects where you are truly an expert, so you don’t feel any fear or hesitation while talking to the client.

Remember, the client is mainly looking to see how well you understand their work or problem and how confident you are in delivering it. Only after that comes the payment.

A wealthy client doesn’t worry about money — they focus on results. Give results, get paid.

Learn Gaming Skills

Gaming is a billion-dollar industry. If you play games without any purpose, it’s just a waste of time. But if you make gaming your profession, you can earn money through it, and becoming a freelancer becomes easier too.

Learning to game and actually doing professional gaming are two different fields. If you’re a gamer who plays professionally and also runs a gaming business, it can’t be called a waste of time—provided your gaming is halal and does not involve nudity or obscenity. The more you avoid such things, the better.

Gaming is definitely a challenging field. But if you create your own games, it becomes easier, and you get a chance to become a game developer in a new way. Plus, you can earn extra income through AdSense.

Learn Fighting Skills

Fighting isn’t just about throwing punches and kicks. It’s a skill that makes you mentally and physically stronger. But very few people choose fighting as a freelance skill. Maybe they don’t realize that through fighting skills, you can build your identity as a martial artist both offline and online—and make money from it. You can offer self-defense training, create martial arts videos, lead fitness and stretching sessions, and even teach virtual classes for kids and adults. Plus, by sharing martial arts training videos and tips on YouTube, Instagram, or other platforms, you can earn income through ad revenue and sponsorships. This skill not only brings financial benefits but also helps you stay fit and teach self-defense to others.
