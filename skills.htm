Fiverr Skills

On Fiverr, you create services and then wait for orders to come in so you can complete the work and deliver it to the customer. This approach is entirely different from traditional freelancing and has little to do with the freelancing business model. An active freelancer works directly with clients, while a Fiverr seller provides services based on predefined packages.

On Fiverr, the focus is on setting up your "shop," showcasing services, displaying items like portfolios, and ranking your gigs. The goal is to rank your gig (service) higher so that it appears in front of potential customers. Once they understand your service offering, they can make a decision and place an order.

Many people in Pakistan are introduced to Fiverr, but very few experts honestly explain that Fiverr is not directly related to freelancing in the traditional sense. It is a business platform where you operate as a business owner and deal with customers. However, Fiverr has strict rules against accessing direct customer information, such as private data. Everything must be conducted within the platform.

In other words, Fiverr can feel like a controlled environment where you give up a significant portion of your earnings as commission, do all the work yourself, and Fiverr keeps the client’s data. If Fiverr ever decides to suspend or delete your account, your business could collapse overnight. Therefore, it is essential to understand their rules and regulations before starting your business on the platform.

Freelancing Skills

Freelancing is the art of working independently as a self-employed individual, offering your skills and expertise directly to clients without relying solely on platforms like Fiverr or Upwork. True freelancing is about building your personal brand, networking, and cultivating relationships to secure clients on your own terms. It’s a business where you operate as a service provider, managing everything from client acquisition to project delivery, pricing, and invoicing. This approach provides full control over your work, allowing you to create a sustainable and flexible career while establishing yourself as a trusted professional in your field.
