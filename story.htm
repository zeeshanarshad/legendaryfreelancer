For over 14 years, I thrived as a freelancer working with international clients. But when I returned to Pakistan to uplift aspiring freelancers, I faced unimaginable adversity—professional sabotage, emotional breakdown, and a crisis of faith. Just when I thought it was the end, a divine force intervened...

The Full Story

The Rise

My early freelancing journey was filled with international success, working with top-tier clients and teaching aspiring freelancers through platforms like Udemy.

The Fall

Returning to Pakistan, I faced jealousy and sabotage from society. This led to an emotional and spiritual breakdown, causing me to lose my memory and withdraw from the world.

The Rebirth

In my darkest hour, ALLAH, the Mighty King of the Universe, raised me back to life. With renewed strength and purpose, I reclaimed my legacy and became the Legendary Freelancer of Pakistan.

Discover More

My journey is far from over. Stay tuned as I share insights, lessons, and tools to help you rise above challenges and achieve greatness.
