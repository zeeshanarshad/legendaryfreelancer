The Short Story

The top 1% rated international freelancer of IT industry who achieved this rank in just 7 years by the mercy of Allah. Then he took his freelancing career forward and started teaching international students online through Udemy.

When he turned to Pakistan in 2017 to educate the poor and middle class people and help the owners of Pakistani companies, conspiracies were laid against him and the top and famous people of the industry together pushed him back.

People ignored him and considered him insignificant and illiterate person because of his simplicity and speaking Urdu. Now after his mental death, he has come back as a legendary freelancer with more abilities than before by the Command of Allahﷻ

Indeed, Allah alone is enough to help His servant. Allahu Akbar
