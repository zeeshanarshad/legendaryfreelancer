Resurrections with ALLAH (اللہ)

The purpose of this project is to bring the dead back to life in this world.
For this mission, the friends of ALLAH (Awliya Allah) will step forward.
Investors will contribute their wealth.
Everyone will come together and pray to ALLAH.
ALLAH will bring the dead back to life.
Indeed, He has full power to give life to the dead.

Who ALLAH chooses to revive or not — that decision belongs to ALLAH alone.
The duty of Muslims is to make an effort and trust in ALLAH.

Since I myself have, with the help of ALLAH, already revived the dead,
this is not something so extraordinary that it should be considered impossible.

Now, there is a need to work on a larger scale so that non-Muslims around the world can witness how powerful ALLAH truly is —
and that all the disbelievers of the world are helpless in this matter —
they cannot even bring a single dead mosquito back to life after it has dried out.

World of Humancraft

A system will be developed to work in the real world and upgrade characters just like in World of Warcraft.
Under this system, gamers will focus on their personal development.
They will learn high-level skills, discover their hidden talents, purchase skills from various trainers, and gain mastery through practice.

They will work in the real world, help people, start their own businesses, and engage in physical-world gaming.

For their efforts, they will receive rewards, awards, and achievements, so they can continue progressing by completing quests.

Whether playing solo or in teams to complete a task, this will be real-world gaming inspired by World of Warcraft.
But this time, the character will be their own real self — stepping out of the virtual world to play the game in the physical world, using the powers of ALLAH to their full potential.

Spiritual Wireless Hospitals

Establishment of Spiritual Wireless Hospitals Across Pakistan.
There will be no need for physical medical doctors for treatment here.
Instead, incurable patients will receive spiritual operations for healing.

For the treatment, cooperation will be sought from Pakistan’s Maulana Abrar Alam and Shah Yaqeeq Baba.
Both will use all their abilities, powers, jinn, spiritual helpers, and other resources to help incurable patients worldwide,
and people will begin to find relief from their illnesses.

Medical equipment can be used for immediate care, medicines can be given, and ventilator machines can be operated,
but the real healing work will be done spiritually by a religious scholar and Waliullah (friend of ALLAH).

If major hospitals want to provide treatment under this spiritual wireless system or establish a department for it,
that can also be done.
This will make it easier by removing the need to build separate hospitals.

For further cooperation, Maulana Abrar Alam can be contacted through his website.
