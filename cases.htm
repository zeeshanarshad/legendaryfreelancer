Life Transformations

The legend has no power to give any benefit or cause harm to anyone without Allah's permission. The legend only makes his sincere effort, but the final outcome comes only from Allah, the One and Only, the Supreme. His kingdom and dominion belong to Him alone. Allah is the Greatest.

Spiritual Treatment

All incurable creatures (humans, jinn, wild animals, birds, insects, and others) receive healing by the command of ALLAH. Reciting Quranic verses, blowing from a distance, praying, giving charity, etc., are only means. When a person makes an effort, improvement happens by ALLAH’s command. How much healing ALLAH grants through whom depends on that person’s actions, piety, righteousness, and intention. No one can benefit or harm anyone unless ALLAH wills it.
