ہم نے اس کا صحیح فیصلہ سلیمان کو سمجھا دیا۔ ہاں ہر ایک کو ہم نے حکم وعلم دے رکھا تھا اور داؤد کے تابع ہم نے پہاڑ کر دیئے تھے جو تسبیح کرتےتھے اور پرند بھی۔ ہم کرنے والے ہی تھے۔

Misuse of music is wrong just as misuse of a knife makes it a crime. If music is used properly during the right tasks, a person can perform many tasks that he is unable to perform in normal circumstances and times of low emotional energies and weak mental state. Therefore, it is important to gain knowledge about where to use music, how to use it, and when to use it, otherwise the same music listening habit can seriously harm the mindset of a person with addiction of music. Just as if someone does not know the treatment for snake venom, then while playing with dangerous snakes, he can die from the bites of snakes. So...it is better that only an expert play with dangerous snakes. Only those who can save themselves from being killed by its venom should listen music, otherwise it is better to avoid it.
